Values statement

At Nameleon, we are guided by a set of core values that underpin our mission to protect and preserve the environment for the benefit of current and future generations. These values shape our actions, decisions, and relationships, and serve as the foundation of our work:
Trust:
We recognize and honor the trust placed in us by our partners, governments, private entities, and donors. We are committed to maintaining and strengthening this trust by consistently upholding the highest standards of integrity, honesty, and transparency in all our activities.
Collaboration:
We believe that solving environmental challenges requires a collective effort. We actively seek partnerships and collaborations with government agencies, civil societies, businesses, and other stakeholders to develop innovative and sustainable solutions that address the complex issues we face.
Accountability:
We take responsibility for our actions and are accountable to our partners, stakeholders, and the communities we serve. We strive for excellence in everything we do, ensuring that our projects and initiatives are implemented effectively and efficiently to achieve meaningful and lasting impact.
Science-based Approach:
We are committed to evidence-based decision-making and rely on rigorous scientific research and expertise to guide our strategies and solutions. We value scientific integrity and work closely with researchers and experts to ensure that our efforts are grounded in sound science.
Imagination and Perseverance:
We understand that the conservation of our environment requires creativity, innovation, and determination. We embrace these qualities, inspiring and fostering imagination within our organization and in the broader community. We persevere in the face of challenges, continually seeking new approaches to protect and restore our natural world.
Leadership:
We strive to be leaders in the field of environmental conservation. Our staff and volunteers embody leadership by example, actively engaging in initiatives that promote sustainable practices, environmental stewardship, and advocacy for positive change. We aim to inspire others to join us in our mission and become agents of environmental preservation.
As an environmental non-profit organization, we are deeply committed to upholding these values and ensuring that our actions align with our principles. The world demands no less from us, and we are dedicated to meeting this demand as we work towards a sustainable and thriving future for all.

Our objectives

By pursuing these objectives, Nameleon aims to contribute significantly to the protection, restoration, and sustainable management of the Namib Desert's ecological integrity, while also fostering environmental education, awareness, and cultural appreciation among different audiences.
