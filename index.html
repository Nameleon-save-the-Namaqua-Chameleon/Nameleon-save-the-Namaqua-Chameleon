„ Save the Namaqua Chameleon Trust ”

Background of the organisation

Nameleon is an environmental non-profit organization dedicated to the preservation and protection of the unique and fragile ecosystem of the Namib Desert in Namibia. We were founded with the mission to address the urgent issue of the declining population of Namaqua chameleons, a vital species within this desert biome.

The Namaqua chameleons play a crucial role in controlling the population of tenebroid beetles, a species whose proliferation could have detrimental effects on the delicate balance of the desert ecosystem. Recognizing the significance of these chameleons and the potential ecological consequences if they were to disappear, we have initiated efforts to raise awareness among road users in areas such as the roads to Sesriem out of Walvis Bay and the Boshua Pass exit out of Swakop towards Langer Heinrich mine.

Our founder, Mr. Marlon Schultz, is a nature conservationist with extensive experience as a tour guide in the Namib, has witnessed firsthand the importance of preserving the Namib Desert and its diverse fauna and flora. With Namibia already experiencing a climate crisis, we recognize the vulnerability of the micro fauna and flora in this region, making their preservation a critical concern.

In addition to our initial focus on chameleon conservation, our long-term vision encompasses monitoring the movement and well-being of key fauna and flora species in the Namib Desert. By closely observing their behavior and habitats, we aim to gain valuable insights into the impacts of climate change and other environmental factors on this fragile ecosystem.

Education and awareness play pivotal roles in our organization's approach. We believe that the youth are our future stewards of the environment. Through educative tourism and outreach programs, we strive to empower and educate young people about the importance of conservation and sustainable practices. By instilling a sense of responsibility and respect for the sensitive desert ecology, we aim to cultivate a generation of environmental advocates who will champion the preservation of the Namib Desert for years to come.

At Nameleon, we are driven by our passion for nature, our commitment to preserving the Namib Desert, and our belief in the power of education. By working collaboratively with local communities, government agencies, and other stakeholders, we aspire to create a sustainable future where the unique biodiversity and ecological balance of the Namib Desert can thrive, ensuring its preservation for generations ahead.
